Our Services

Six pillars of practice

Advisory, research, education, mentorship, consultation, and community — six pillars that guide our work across disciplines, cultures, and communities.

CoAdvisory

Strategic Advisory

Advisory work across health innovation, digital health, research translation, and service development. Supporting organisations, startups, and institutions to build thoughtful, evidence-informed, and people-centred solutions.

Who is this for?

Organisations, startups, institutions, and government bodies seeking strategic guidance in health innovation, digital health, and service design.

What we offer

  • Health innovation strategy
  • Digital health advisory
  • Research translation consulting
  • Service design and development

CoLab

Research & Collaboration

Research, collaboration, and knowledge sharing across telehealth, rehabilitation, occupation-centred practice, and meaningful participation. Connecting people, ideas, and disciplines across borders.

Who is this for?

Researchers, clinicians, and academic institutions looking to collaborate on meaningful research and knowledge exchange.

What we offer

  • Collaborative research projects
  • Knowledge sharing across disciplines
  • Cross-border research partnerships
  • Research mentoring
Explore collaboration →

CoAcademy

Education & Training

Workshops, teaching, and training in occupation-centred practice, digital health, clinical reasoning, and inclusive approaches to care and education.

Who is this for?

Clinicians, educators, students, and organisations seeking to strengthen practice through learning and professional development.

What we offer

  • Occupation-centred practice workshops
  • Digital health training
  • Clinical reasoning programs
  • Inclusive care education
View upcoming programs →

CoAspire

Mentorship & Development

Mentorship and professional development for students and emerging professionals. Supporting reflective, confident, and globally minded growth in practice, research, and leadership.

Who is this for?

Students, emerging professionals, and early-career researchers looking for guidance and growth opportunities.

What we offer

  • One-on-one mentorship
  • Career development guidance
  • Research skill building
  • Global practice exposure

CoPractice

Consultation

Occupation-centred consultation supporting individuals, families, and organisations to navigate meaningful participation in everyday life. Offering professional guidance grounded in evidence-informed practice and cross-cultural understanding.

Who is this for?

Individuals, families, and organisations seeking occupation-centred guidance to support meaningful participation.

What we offer

  • Occupation-centred consultation
  • Professional guidance for meaningful participation
  • Cross-cultural practice consultation
Book a consultation →

CoCommunity

Professional Community & Global Collaboration

Supporting global professional communities through mentorship, voluntary leadership, and international collaboration. Connecting clinicians, researchers, students, and organisations across borders to strengthen occupational therapy and rehabilitation practice.

Who is this for?

Clinicians, researchers, students, educators, and organisations looking to connect, collaborate, and grow within the global occupational therapy and rehabilitation community.

What we offer

  • Voluntary leadership and community mentorship
  • International collaboration and knowledge exchange
  • Cross-border professional networking and support

Some activities presented here include voluntary contributions provided without fee. Not all are formal CoColour Life initiatives; some reflect Yuho Okita's personal professional and community involvement.
