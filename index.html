CoColour Life brings together practice, education, and innovation to support meaningful participation in everyday life.

CoColour Life is a platform for practice, education, research, and collaboration, grounded in a commitment to meaningful participation in everyday life. We believe that meaningful occupation can transform how people participate, connect, and thrive. Through practice, education, research, and partnership, we work with individuals, organisations, and communities to create inclusive and practical solutions.

We work across cultures, disciplines, and borders to support innovation in health, rehabilitation, and meaningful participation.

Our Purpose

From monochrome to colour

Our Story

Many people live in monochrome — disconnected from the occupations that bring meaning, colour, and connection to everyday life. Illness, injury, displacement, and life circumstances can limit meaningful participation, leaving people feeling isolated, unseen, or without purpose.

CoColour Life was built on the belief that it does not have to stay this way. Through occupation-centred practice, research, education, and collaboration, we work to help people, organisations, and communities reconnect with the occupations that bring vibrancy, belonging, and identity to life.

Mission

To enable meaningful participation through occupation-centred practice, research, education, and collaboration.

Vision

A world where every person can access the occupations that bring colour, meaning, and connection to life — regardless of geography, ability, or circumstance.

Values

We are guided by collaboration, diversity, evidence-informed practice, innovation, and humanity.
