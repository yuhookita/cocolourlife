About

Bringing colour to
meaningful participation

CoColour Life is a research, education, consulting, mentorship, and practice platform dedicated to enabling meaningful participation in life.

Founded on the belief that meaningful occupation has the power to transform participation, identity, and wellbeing, we work across clinical practice, research, professional education, and strategic consulting to help people, organisations, and communities thrive.

Operating internationally across Australia and Asia, we bridge cultures, disciplines, and perspectives to create collaborative solutions for complex challenges in health, rehabilitation, and human participation.

Our Purpose

From monochrome to colourful

Our Story

Many people live in monochrome — disconnected from the occupations that give life meaning, colour, and connection. Whether through illness, injury, displacement, or circumstance, the loss of meaningful participation can leave people feeling invisible, isolated, and without purpose.

CoColour Life was born from the conviction that it doesn't have to be this way. Through occupation-centred practice, collaborative research, education, and innovation, we work to help people rediscover the occupations that bring vibrancy, belonging, and identity back into their lives.

Mission

To enable meaningful participation in life through occupation-centred research, education, collaboration, and clinical practice that bridges cultures, disciplines, and communities.

Vision

A world where every person can access the occupations that bring colour, meaning, and connection to their life — regardless of geography, ability, or circumstance.

Values

Collaboration. Diversity. Evidence. Innovation. Humanity. We believe in the transformative power of meaningful occupation and the strength of working together.

Education & Workshops

Learning that transforms practice

Professional development, workshops, and training designed to build communities of practice and translate knowledge into real-world impact.

CO-OP Workshops

Comprehensive training in the Cognitive Orientation to daily Occupational Performance (CO-OP) approach. These workshops equip clinicians with the knowledge and skills to implement evidence-based, goal-directed, occupation-centred practice.

Delivered internationally, our CO-OP workshops are more than technical training — they are opportunities to build communities of practice, foster cross-cultural dialogue, and strengthen the global network of occupation-centred practitioners.

Founder

Yuho Okita

Occupational therapist, researcher, educator, and consultant working at the intersection of clinical practice, research, education, and health innovation.

Yuho brings a unique global perspective to occupation-centred practice, drawing on deep experience across both Australian and Japanese healthcare systems. Through CoColour Life, Yuho integrates clinical expertise, research inquiry, professional mentorship, and strategic consulting to create impact across multiple levels — from individual lives to healthcare systems and international communities of practice.
